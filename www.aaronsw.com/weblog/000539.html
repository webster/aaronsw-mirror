Shrub Wants to Stop Wildfires by Stopping Forests

Sorry for all the political announcements, but this one was too crazy to pass up. Heritage Forests Campaign has a letter-sending service to tell your senators to stop the Shrub’s “Healthy Forests Initiative” which basically eliminates all the environmental protections for forests we’ve had, in the name of trying to protect them from wildfires. It’s absurd and you should let your representatives know that.
