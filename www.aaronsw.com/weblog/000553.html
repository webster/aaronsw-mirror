Books Consume Life

I’m the kind of crazy web-head who finds replacing paper book marks by storing current page numbers on a remote web-accessible-server I don’t control but can get my data out of via XML appealing. That’s probably why I like All Consuming so much.

All Consuming is the weblogger’s book portal. It keeps track of the most mentioned books, let’s you post commentary on books via TrackBack and has a web/db set-up which lets you keep track of which books you’ve purchased, read and are reading, with export feeds in XML and RSS.

For now I’ll point you to the page for Emergence, the book I read this weekend.
