final(?) segway update

Well, it’s almost time to return the Segway and we’ve all but lost interest in it. Working on the movie kept us going for a little while longer but since I posted it the Segway has pretty much stayed in its place in the corner, off. I think we’ve explored everything there is to do with it, and now it’s just uninteresting.

It’s not like we have much of a practical use for it, especially not now. It’s so cold and snowy outside that every time we go out we have to suit up in so much clothing that we’d be protected from a nuclear blast. And it’s not like we have anyplace to go: everything’s closed for Xmas and school’s out.

On a related note, San Francisco has banned the Segway. Sigh, the one city where it might actually be useful.
