More Crazy Haxies

The crazy people over at unsanity have put together some more haxies for OS X including metalifizer which can add or remove the brushed metal look to all your programs, a Jaguar-compatible FruitMenu, and ClearDock, which lets you change the dock background or remove it. They’ve also released Application Enhancer, the backend that powers all the haxies.
