Hack Yo’ Mac

Marc Liyanage has a great list of Mac OS X customization tools, I’ve found myself downloading a bunch of them to try on his recommendation… I’ll see how they work out.

The coolest thing I learned about were the pbpaste and pbcopy command-line commands which let you use the clipboard (pasteboard) to move things between the command-line and the rest of your Mac.
