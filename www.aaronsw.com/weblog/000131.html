US Congress: Universal Military Training

US Congress: Universal Military Training and Service Act of 2001: To require the induction into the Armed Forces of young men registered under the Military Selective Service Act, and to authorize young women to volunteer, to receive basic military training and education for a period of up to one year.

They’re trying to reinstate the draft! Currently under discussion by the House Committee on Armed Services. The Bill was introduced on the 20th of December, 2001. How come there’s been no media coverage?
