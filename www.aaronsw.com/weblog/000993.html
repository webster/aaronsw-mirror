Scott McCloud is Back!

Scott McCloud is one of my favorite writers and his medium, comic books, simply adds to the joy of his work.

He’s released his new comic, The Right Number, under a new micropayment system, BitPass. (The comic costs a quarter.) I haven’t read the comic but from what I’ve heard, it’s going to be good.

Even better, his comicblog (a website with a new comic every day), Morning Improv is coming back soon. This is going to be fun.
