Ideal PDA

Everyone’s getting a Hiptop/Sidekick, but it doesn’t seem to have all the things I need. I will buy a WearableGizmo (i.e. something that fits in my pocket) if it has:

  • a reasonably-large screen
  • a usable interface
  • a cell-phone Internet connection
  • Web client
  • IMAP mail client
  • SSH client

Ideally, it will also have:

  • a cell phone
  • IRC client
  • high-quality camera
  • 802.11 support
  • support for third-party apps
  • support for third-party apps in Python

Does such a thing exist? I doubt it… maybe I should just stay with my laptop.
