growth spurt

I’m having a growth spurt.

It’s weird to feel yourself growing. You look around, and everythings a little lower than it was a few minutes ago. You stretch, and your muscles don’t contract. And you stretch a lot. Nobody else seems to notice.

The worst part is the hunger. You wake up starving. You go to sleep starving. In the middle you stuff yourself with so much food that you can’t swallow one more bite, and then an hour later you’re starving again. It’s impossible to get any work that requires concentration done. You flail aimlessly.

I hope it ends soon.
